Experiment Highlights

Sonic Destruction Research

Feedback Ritual with Veo 3: Exploring how AI can capture the raw, destructive energy of noise rock performance. This experiment uses Veo 3 to channel the intensity of a Thai woman artist destroying her guitar in a typical noise music concert setting — investigating how cultural expressions of rebellion and sonic destruction translate through generative AI. The piece examines the intersection of traditional performance art, contemporary noise culture, and AI's ability to understand and recreate moments of creative chaos.

Micro-Narrative Experiments with Hailuo AI 02

10-Second Cinematic Storytelling: A deep dive into the potential of ultra-short form video generation using Hailuo AI 02's text-to-video capabilities. These experiments feature a badass Thai high school girl protagonist slaying monsters through heavy editing and sound design. Working within the constraint of just 10 seconds of generated footage, the project explores how post-production can transform AI-generated clips into compelling micro-narratives that capture the essence of action cinema and contemporary Asian youth culture's relationship with fantasy and power.
