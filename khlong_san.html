CONTEXT_DATA
Khlong San is a constructed city based on real urban references from Bangkok, Ho Chi Minh, Manila, and Jakarta. Flooded alleys, plastic chairs, gold jewelry, underground fights, street markets, and neon-lit temples shape its look. It blends the sacred and the everyday—floods are normal, fireworks explode over concrete rooftops, and kids ride motorcycles through shrine-filled backstreets.
VISUAL_PROCESS
All visuals are AI-generated using Midjourney v7. But instead of stylized perfection, the focus is on imperfect textures—blurred shots, flash burns, handheld angles. Every frame is designed to resemble street photography, film stills, or documentary footage. The characters often don't look at the camera. They just exist inside this fictional city, shaped by its tension and beauty.
SUBJECT_ANALYSIS
The people of Khlong San are mostly women—seen in subways, alleyways, or standing in floodwaters. Many wear traditional elements mixed with modern grit: grillz, gold chains, ritual headpieces, cheap plastic, and sequins. Their expressions are either passive or unbothered. Nothing is staged. Every image acts like a found moment.
